YesEuropeLab
Europe needs you!

Join the Movement!


Our European Union has reached a critical crossroads in its history. The situation demands a bold reaction and mobilization from all corners of the continent. We are calling women and men with little to no prior political experience. You can self-declare, if you up for the challenge, nominate anyone or get involved in the movement. Candidates can be from any party or no party at all, and the office sought can be at the national, state, or local level.


Millions of Europeans have imagined themselves on the campaign trail but few know where to begin and what it really takes to run for and win public office. YesEuropeLab online platform and an accompanying online reality show provides an inside look at the challenges, excitement, frustration, power-struggles, and far-less glamorous elements of a political race and public office holding. Viewers will cheer on their favorites as candidates receive resources and mentorship needed to succeed.

About Us


We are a group of European students and fellows based at Harvard working with media professionals and political consultant. We believe that our European Union is threatened by an unprecedented set of challenges and reactionary forces. Our goal is to empower European citizens from across the continent to believe that they can be part of the solution, re-energize the electorate and to launch the next generation of European political leaders from the grassroots.
