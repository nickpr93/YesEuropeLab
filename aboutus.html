Our Amazing Team

We are a group of students and fellows from Harvard Harvard College and Kennedy School students and fellows working with media professionals and political consultants. We believe fervently in our nation's promise that "We the People" are responsible for a more perfect union.

Nicolas Miailhe

Co-Founder


Nicolas Miaihe is a mid-career MPA student at the Harvard Kennedy School and an Arthur Sachs Scholar. Trained as a political scientist, he brings his expertise in strategy and digital empowerment. Nicolas has over 10 years of experience working at the crossroads of government, high-technology and society, particularly in emerging markets. He is the Founding Associate of People for Global Transformation, a high-level international working-group which gathers fifteen global leaders.

Scott Jacobsen

Co-Founder


Scott Jacobsen is a mid-career MPA student at Harvard Kennedy School and an award-winning communications and social media expert. He has worked for Republican, Democrat, and Independent U.S. Senators and has a thorough understanding of how to navigate the political landscape. He is the Founder of the social enterprise DoneGood, a past arts educator, and received extensive theatrical training in playwriting, directing, and acting as an undergrad.

Tom Schmittzehe

Casting and Development


Tom is a Mid-Career MPA student at the Harvard Kennedy School and an Arthur Sachs Scholar. Tom is a serial social entrepreneur, mentor to start-ups and impact investor - with a focus on crowdsourcing platforms. He originated and helped produce an award-winning hit docu-series in China, featuring the journeys of grass-root entrepreneurs there. Tom has an MBA from the Wharton Business School; and has been actively involved in politics and public policy in his home-country - the UK - for 15 years, where he has been a policy advisor and campaign manager.

Our goal is to empower citizens to believe that they can be part of the solution, re-energize the electorate and to launch the next generation of political leaders from the grassroots.
